ISO management system (MSS) is a set of interrelated elements that an organization uses to establish its policies and objectives, and to achieve those objectives by planning, implementing, and controlling processes effectively.

HSSE

HSSE stands for Health, Safety, Security, and Environment. It is a management system that aims to protect the health and safety of employees, the security of assets, and the environment. HSSE is important for all organizations, but it is especially critical for organizations that operate in hazardous or high-risk environments.

Human Resources Management

Human resources management (HRM) is the strategic and coherent approach to the effective and efficient management of people in a company or organization such that they help their business gain a competitive advantage. It is designed to maximize employee performance in service of an employer's strategic objectives.
